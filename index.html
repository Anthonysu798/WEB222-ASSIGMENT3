RICE AND BEANS RECIPE

Now let us dive into the recipe so you can learn to make rice and bean at home.

Here are the ingredients that you need to successful made rice and bean

  • First, you need Red Kidney Beans you can find in any grocery store
  • 1 table spoon of salt
  • 1 cup of canned coconut Milk so that you can add more flavor to the rice and bean also it taste much more better.
  • 1/2 tablespoon of black pepper so you can get the chilli taste
  • 2 lbs of white rice
  • 1 Whole Onion and some garlics
  • You need 6-8 cups of water for steam the rice and bean

You have prepare all the ingredients now it’s time for cooking

  1. You need to soak the Red kidney bean using the 6-8 cups of water until it is soften or else you won’t able to threw with your teeth.
  2. Now cut the onions and garlic into pieces and boil the bean until it is soften.
  3. Now seasons the beans with black pepper.
  4. Pure the canned coconut milk into the beans then stir and let it boil.
  5. Which is the final step, add rice to the season beans and cook the rice in low heat.
  6. The rice will be ready when all the water is absorbed and rice is soften.

History of Rice and Bean

How to make dish Healthier?

Most traditional rice and bean recipes are frequently cook with ham, bacon, etc. This add flavor to the meal and make it taste delicious. However, these kinds of meat have a lot of fat and a lot of sodium which make the food tasty but unhealthy to the body.

How to choose the rice?

Rice, while a staple in many cuisines worldwide, is not without its considerations, particularly when it comes to health. One consequence about rice is its arsenic content. You may ask what is right? Arsenic is a heavy metal found naturally in the environment and absorbed by rice from the soil. Consistent and regular exposure to even small amounts of arsenic can amplify the risk of certain cancers and heart diseases.

How to reduce Arsenic content when cooking rice?

Reduce arsenic content when cooking rice meaning save your life in the long run from cancers and heart diseases. So how to reduce arsenic content? By simply add more water into the rice and let it cook in a steamer or rice cooker.
